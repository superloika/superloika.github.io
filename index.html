Hi, I'm Kaloy!

I fell in love with computer programming since the early part of my high school days. I wrote code just for fun but as I've read articles and tutorials on the internet, it gradually made me even more curious. I tried to solve some famous problems like how to generate fibonacci sequence, sort an array, and a lot more. It became part of my daily routine. Instead of playing computer games like the other kids do, I wrote code. It was fun but overwhelming sometimes specially when I'm stucked with an annoying bug. C/C++ and Visual Basic was my first two languages by the way. I dug deeper into programming and tried to learn other languages.

I finished my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science at Bohol Island State University, Balilihan, Bohol, Philippines. I studied Software Engineering, System Analysis and Design, Web Development, Mobile Application Development, and Graphic Design. I am not a pro in terms of programming, I just love it. For me, it is not just a skill, it is more than that.. it's a passion. Knowing how to code is something like possessing a superpower. It makes you stand out amongst others. Gabe Newell once said, "The programmers of today are the future wizards of tomorrow".
